Instructions

  • Make sure that "Enhance pointer precision" is off.
  • Update the "in-game sensitivity" to your preference.
  • Pick a weapon, mag size, and attachment level.
  • Click anywhere on the area below.
  • Get a high score by compensating a recoil.

Enable Show path to see the ideal compensation trajectory.

Enable Show pacer to see a running dot to follow (works only if "show path" is on).

Auto toggle hint will switch between hint and no hint modes between sprays.

Middle button toggles the trace display mode.

I recommend "Show path", "Show pacer" and "Auto toggle hint" enabled.

Enable "Real trace" to use one of the real recorded traces instead of a fixed average.

Scores are counted separately for different setups.
